Computer Vision in Advertising and Marketing

1st CVAM Workshop @ ICCV 2025
📅 Workshop Date: Oct 20th 2025 (During ICCV 2025)
📍 Location: In-person at ICCV 2025. Honolulu, Hawaii
🔗 Workshop Website

🌟 Overview

The CVAM workshop aims to bridge the gap between academic research and industry needs in the rapidly evolving advertising and marketing sector. With over $600B in annual spend, advertising remains a visually rich domain that is still underrepresented in the computer vision research community. From brand safety and contextual targeting to creative generation and performance forecasting, CVAM will explore how modern computer vision and generative AI can drive innovation across the advertising value chain.

We invite high-quality paper submissions addressing novel research, systems, and applications at the intersection of computer vision, marketing optimization, and advertising technology.

📌 Key Topics

  • Multimodal understanding of visual and textual ad content
  • Real-time bidding and conversion rate prediction
  • Brand safety, logo/product detection, and visual brand consistency
  • Generative AI for ad personalization and content optimization
  • Privacy-preserving analytics and responsible AI in marketing
